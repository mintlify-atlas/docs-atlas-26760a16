## Customer Types

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Registered Customers">
    Customers who created an account through your booking app:
    
    - Full profile information
    - Email and phone verified
    - Can make their own bookings
    - Receive booking confirmations and reminders
    - Cannot be deleted (only blacklisted)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Merchant-Added Customers">
    Customers you manually add to the system:
    
    - Created by you for walk-in or phone bookings
    - Limited information (as much as you collect)
    - Marked with "Customer Added by You" badge
    - Can be edited or deleted
    - Can have bookings transferred to other customers
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Creating Customers

Add new customers to your database:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Customers">
    Click "Customers" in the sidebar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click New Customer">
    Select the "New" button in the customers view
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Information">
    Fill in customer details:
    - First name (required)
    - Last name (required)
    - Email (optional)
    - Phone number (optional)
    - Birthday (optional)
    - Notes (optional)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Customer is added to your database and available for booking
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Blacklist Management

<Warning>
  Blacklisting prevents customers from creating new bookings through the customer app.
</Warning>

### Blacklisting Customers

Restrict problematic customers from booking:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Customer Menu">
    Click the three-dots menu on the customer profile
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Blacklist">
    Choose "Blacklist Customer" option
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Provide Reason">
    Enter a reason for the blacklist (required for your records)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Confirm">
    Customer is immediately blacklisted
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Effects of Blacklisting

- Customer cannot create new bookings through the app
- Existing bookings remain active
- Customer still appears in your database
- Marked with red "Blacklisted" badge
- You can still create bookings for them manually

### Removing from Blacklist

<Accordion title="Unban Process">
  
  1. Open blacklisted customer's profile
  2. Click three-dots menu
  3. Select "Unban customer"
  4. Customer can immediately book again
  
  The original blacklist reason is preserved in the customer record.
  
</Accordion>

## Viewing Blacklisted Customers

Access your blacklist:

1. Navigate to Customers page
2. Click the "Blacklist" tab
3. View all blacklisted customers with reasons

## Transferring Bookings

<Info>
  Only available for merchant-added customers with existing bookings.
</Info>

Consolidate duplicate customer records:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="When to Transfer">
    - Discovered duplicate customer entries
    - Customer requests profile consolidation
    - Correcting data entry errors
    - Merging temporary and permanent profiles
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Transfer Process">
    1. Open the customer profile to transfer FROM
    2. Click three-dots menu → "Transfer customer"
    3. Select the target customer to transfer TO
    4. Confirm transfer
    5. All bookings move to the target customer
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="After Transfer">
    - All bookings now linked to target customer
    - Original customer profile remains
    - Can now delete the empty profile
    - Booking history preserved
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Deleting Customers

<Warning>
  Only merchant-added customers without bookings can be deleted. Registered customers cannot be deleted.
</Warning>

Delete a customer:

1. Ensure customer has no bookings (or transfer them first)
2. Click three-dots menu on customer profile
3. Select "Delete Customer"
4. Confirm deletion

<Note>
  Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Consider blacklisting instead if you might need the record later.
</Note>
