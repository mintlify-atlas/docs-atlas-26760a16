The merchant dashboard is your central command center, providing at-a-glance visibility into your business metrics and upcoming activities.

## Overview

Access comprehensive business analytics and stay informed about your daily operations. The dashboard automatically refreshes to show you the most relevant data for managing your reservation business.

<img src="/images/dashboard-overview.png" alt="Dashboard Overview" />

## Key Metrics

Track essential business indicators over customizable time periods:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Revenue Tracking" icon="dollar-sign">
    Monitor total earnings from completed bookings with percentage change indicators showing growth trends.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Booking Count" icon="calendar-check">
    View the total number of completed bookings and track how this compares to previous periods.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Cancellation Rate" icon="ban">
    Keep tabs on customer cancellations to identify potential issues or trends.
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Average Duration" icon="clock">
    Understand typical service lengths to optimize your scheduling and capacity planning.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Time Period Selection

Analyze your performance over different timeframes:

- **Last 7 days**: Quick view of recent activity
- **Last 30 days**: Monthly performance overview

All metrics automatically update when you switch between time periods, including the revenue chart and percentage change indicators.

## Revenue Chart

<img src="/images/revenue-chart.png" alt="Revenue Chart" />

Visualize your revenue trends with an interactive area chart that displays:

- Daily revenue breakdown
- Hover tooltips showing exact amounts for each day
- Trend visualization to spot growth patterns
- Automatic date formatting based on the selected period

## Upcoming Bookings

<Accordion title="View upcoming appointments">
  Stay ahead of your schedule with a dedicated section showing:
  
  - Customer name (or "Walk-in" for anonymous bookings)
  - Service name with color-coded badges
  - Date and time details
  - Quick actions: View in calendar, cancel, or mark as complete
  - Customer notes (expandable when available)
  
  Click any booking to view full details or manage the appointment directly from the dashboard.
</Accordion>

## Latest Bookings

Review recently created bookings to track incoming appointments. This list includes the same details and actions as upcoming bookings, giving you visibility into your booking pipeline.

## Low Stock Alerts

<Warning>
  The dashboard automatically alerts you when product inventory falls below optimal levels.
</Warning>

When products used in your services run low, you'll see:

- Product name and current stock level
- Visual indicator of how many items remain
- Quick link to the products page to restock

This helps prevent service disruptions and ensures you're always prepared for customer appointments.

## Empty States

<Info>
  When you're just getting started, the dashboard displays helpful empty states guiding you to create your first bookings.
</Info>

## Dashboard Actions

Perform quick actions directly from the dashboard:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Cancel Booking">
    Click the trash icon next to any booking to open the cancellation dialog. You can provide a cancellation reason that will be stored with the booking record.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="View in Calendar">
    Click the calendar icon to jump directly to that booking's date in the full calendar view.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Mark Complete">
    Click the checkmark icon to confirm a booking has been completed (coming soon).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Responsive Design

The dashboard automatically adapts to your screen size:

- **Mobile**: Shows 2 key metrics (Revenue and Bookings)
- **Tablet**: Displays 3 metrics
- **Desktop**: Shows all 4 metrics for complete visibility
