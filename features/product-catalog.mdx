## Services Overview

Services are the core offerings customers can book. Each service defines what you provide, how long it takes, and what it costs.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Service Types" icon="briefcase">
    - **1-on-1 Appointments**: Individual customer services
    - **Group Classes**: Multi-participant sessions
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Service Features" icon="list-check">
    - Customizable duration and pricing
    - Multi-phase service workflows
    - Product usage tracking
    - Category organization
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Creating Services

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="1-on-1 Service">
    Create individual appointment services:
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click New Service">
        Navigate to Services → New Service → 1-on-1 service
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Basic Information">
        - **Name**: Service display name
        - **Description**: Details shown to customers
        - **Category**: Organize into service groups
        - **Color**: Visual identifier in calendar
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Pricing">
        - **Price**: Customer cost
        - **Cost**: Your cost (for profit tracking)
        - **Price Type**: Fixed or variable pricing
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Duration & Phases">
        - Set total service duration
        - Or break into multiple phases (e.g., consultation, treatment, follow-up)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Scheduling Settings">
        - **Booking window**: How far in advance customers can book
        - **Cancel deadline**: Cancellation cutoff time
        - **Buffer time**: Gap between bookings
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Product Usage">
        Link products consumed during this service
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Group Service">
    Create classes or group sessions:
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click New Service">
        Navigate to Services → New Service → Group Service
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Basic Details">
        Same as 1-on-1: name, description, category, color
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Capacity Settings">
        - **Min participants**: Minimum to run the class
        - **Max participants**: Maximum capacity
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Pricing Model">
        - **Per person**: Each participant pays the price
        - **Fixed**: Total price regardless of participants
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Duration">
        Set single duration (group services don't use phases)
      </Step>
      
      <Step title="Configure Scheduling">
        Same booking window, cancellation, and buffer settings
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Service Phases

<Info>
  Break complex services into distinct phases for better time management and customer communication.
</Info>

### Phase Types

<Accordion title="Active Phases">
  
  **Working Time**
  - Direct customer interaction
  - Counted toward total service time
  - Visible to customers
  
  Examples: Consultation, Treatment, Training
  
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Buffer Phases">
  
  **Preparation Time**
  - Setup or cleanup between customers
  - Not charged to customer
  - Prevents back-to-back scheduling
  
  Examples: Room setup, Equipment cleaning
  
</Accordion>

### Managing Phases

<img src="/images/service-phases.png" alt="Service Phases Configuration" />

For each phase, configure:
- Phase name
- Duration (in minutes)
- Phase type (active or buffer)
- Sequence order

Phases run consecutively during the booking time block.

## Service Categories

Organize services into logical groups:

### Creating Categories

<Steps>
  <Step title="Click New Category">
    From Services page, click "New category"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Name Category">
    Enter a descriptive category name (e.g., "Hair Services", "Fitness Classes")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Category appears in your service list
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Category Features

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Organization" icon="folder">
    - Group related services together
    - Shown separately in booking interface
    - Collapsible sections
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Ordering" icon="arrow-down-1-9">
    - Reorder categories with up/down arrows
    - Drag services within categories
    - Custom sequence for customer display
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Uncategorized Services

<Info>
  Services without a category appear in the "Uncategorized" section, which always displays last.
</Info>

## Service Display

Services appear as cards in your service list:

<img src="/images/service-cards.png" alt="Service Cards" />

Each card shows:
- Service name and color indicator
- Duration and price
- Booking type badge (1-on-1 or Group)
- Active/inactive status
- Quick action menu

### Service Actions

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Edit">
    Modify any service details. Changes apply to future bookings only.
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Activate/Deactivate">
    Toggle service availability:
    - **Active**: Available for new bookings
    - **Inactive**: Hidden from customers, existing bookings unaffected
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Reorder">
    Move service up or down within its category
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Delete">
    Remove service permanently (only if no bookings exist)
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Service Settings

### Booking Window

<Accordion title="Minimum Window">
  
  How soon customers can book:
  - Same day (0 hours)
  - 24 hours in advance
  - Custom time period
  
  Prevents last-minute bookings that may be impractical.
  
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Maximum Window">
  
  How far in advance bookings are allowed:
  - 30 days (common)
  - 60 days
  - 90 days
  - Custom limit
  
  Helps with capacity planning and prevents booking too far ahead.
  
</Accordion>

### Cancellation Deadline

Set how late customers can cancel:

- 24 hours before appointment
- 48 hours before
- Custom deadline

Late cancellations outside this window may incur penalties (configured elsewhere).

### Buffer Time

<Info>
  Automatically add gaps between bookings for preparation, cleanup, or travel.
</Info>

Buffer options:
- No buffer (back-to-back bookings)
- 5, 10, 15, or 30 minutes
- Custom duration

Buffer time appears as unavailable in the calendar.

## Products

Track inventory items used during services:

### Product Information

Each product includes:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Basic Details" icon="box">
    - Product name
    - Description
    - Unit of measurement
    - Price (optional)
  </Card>
  
  <Card title="Inventory" icon="chart-line">
    - Maximum stock level
    - Current stock amount
    - Low stock threshold
    - Usage tracking
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Creating Products

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Products">
    Click "Products" in the sidebar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Click New Product">
    Open the new product form
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - Name (e.g., "Shampoo", "Towels")
    - Description
    - Unit (e.g., "bottle", "piece", "ml")
    - Price (your cost)
    - Max amount
    - Current amount
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save">
    Product is added to inventory
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Linking Products to Services

Connect products to services for automatic tracking:

1. Edit a service
2. Scroll to "Product Usage" section
3. Search and select products
4. Set amount used per booking
5. Save service

When the service is booked, inventory automatically decrements.

### Product Table

<img src="/images/products-table.png" alt="Products Inventory Table" />

The products table displays:
- Product name and description
- Current vs. max stock levels
- Price information
- Services that use this product
- Quick edit and delete actions

### Low Stock Alerts

<Warning>
  When product inventory drops below 20% of max amount, alerts appear on the dashboard.
</Warning>

Low stock notifications show:
- Product name
- Current quantity
- Link to restock

Helps prevent service disruptions due to supply shortages.

### Restocking Products

1. Navigate to Products
2. Click edit on the product
3. Update "Current amount" field
4. Save changes

Consider setting up regular restocking schedules for frequently used items.

## Service Pricing

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Fixed Pricing">
    Standard price that never changes:
    
    - Set once, applies to all bookings
    - Simple for customers to understand
    - Best for consistent services
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Variable Pricing">
    Price adjusts based on factors:
    
    - Time of day
    - Day of week
    - Customer type
    - Special promotions
    
    (Advanced pricing coming soon)
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Per-Person Pricing">
    For group services:
    
    - Each participant pays the set price
    - Total revenue scales with attendance
    - Good for classes and workshops
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
